## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information.** When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, phone number, username, password (stored in hashed form), and optionally a profile photo.

**Pet Profile Information.** To use PetRecovery's core features, you may provide information about your pet(s), including the pet's name, species, breed, age, sex, color, distinguishing markings, photographs, microchip number, veterinary information, medical conditions, and behavioral notes.

**Lost & Found Reports.** When you report a lost or found pet, we collect the information you submit in the report, including the location where the pet was last seen or found, date and time, photographs, descriptions, and any reward information you choose to offer.

**Communications.** We collect the content of messages you send through the App's in-app messaging system, communications with our support team, and any feedback, survey responses, or reviews you submit.

**Payment Information.** If you make purchases through the Services (such as premium subscriptions, featured listings, or reward escrow services), payment card details are collected and processed by our third-party payment processor, [PAYMENT PROCESSOR NAME — e.g., "Stripe, Inc." or "Apple App Store / Google Play billing"]. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers. We retain transaction records including the amount, date, and last four digits of your card.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Location Data.** With your permission, we collect your device's precise geolocation (GPS) to: (a) tag the location of lost/found pet reports; (b) show you nearby lost pet alerts; and (c) power our map-based search radius features. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time, though this will limit core functionality of the App.

**Device & Usage Information.** We automatically collect your device type, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, IP address, mobile network information, app version, crash logs, and usage data such as features accessed, screens viewed, search queries, and interaction timestamps.

**Cookies & Similar Technologies.** Our website uses cookies, pixels, and local storage to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, analyze traffic, and measure the effectiveness of our communications. See Section 9 for details on managing cookies.

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Social Sign-On.** If you register or log in using a third-party service (e.g., Google, Apple, or Facebook), we receive the profile information you authorize that service to share, such as your name and email address.
- **Microchip Registries.** With your authorization, we may exchange pet identification data with microchip registry partners to assist in reunification: [MICROCHIP REGISTRY PARTNERS — list any registries you integrate with, e.g., "HomeAgain, AKC Reunite," or state "None at this time"].
- **Shelters & Veterinary Partners.** Participating animal shelters, rescues, and veterinary clinics may submit found-pet reports or confirm reunifications through our partner tools.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. Provide, operate, and maintain the Services, including matching lost pet reports with found pet reports;
2. Broadcast lost pet alerts to users within a relevant geographic radius;
3. Facilitate communication between pet owners, finders, shelters, and volunteers;
4. Process payments, subscriptions, and reward escrow transactions;
5. Verify accounts and prevent fraud, scams (including fake "finder" reward scams), spam, and abuse;

8. Analyze usage trends to improve features, performance, and user experience;
9. Debug, identify, and repair errors in the Services;
10. Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service; and
11. Protect the rights, property, and safety of our users, the public, animals, and the Company.

**Legal Bases (where applicable).** If you are located in a jurisdiction requiring a legal basis for processing (such as the EEA or UK), we process your data on the bases of: contract performance (providing the Services), legitimate interests (safety, fraud prevention, improvement), consent (precise location, marketing), and legal obligation.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal information for money. We share information only as follows:

**4.1 Public Lost & Found Listings.** When you post a lost or found pet report, the information in that report — including pet details, photos, the general location, and your chosen contact method — is **visible to other users and may be publicly viewable** on our website and shareable to social media. Do not include information in a public report that you are not comfortable making public. You may choose to mask your exact contact details and communicate through in-app messaging instead.

**4.2 Other Users.** When you message another user, they will see your username, profile photo, and message content.

**4.3 Shelter, Rescue & Veterinary Partners.** We may share report details with verified animal welfare organizations in the relevant area to aid recovery efforts.

**4.4 Service Providers.** We share information with vendors who perform services on our behalf, including cloud hosting ([HOSTING PROVIDER — e.g., "Amazon Web Services"]), analytics ([ANALYTICS PROVIDERS — e.g., "Google Analytics, Mixpanel"]), push notification delivery, customer support tooling, email/SMS delivery, and payment processing. These providers are contractually restricted from using your data for their own purposes.

**4.5 Legal & Safety.** We may disclose information if we believe in good faith it is required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or necessary to protect the safety of any person or animal, investigate fraud or animal cruelty, or protect our legal rights. This may include cooperating with law enforcement in cases of suspected pet theft or reward fraud.

**4.6 Business Transfers.** If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

**4.7 With Your Consent.** We share information for any other purpose disclosed to you at the time of collection with your consent.

**4.8 Aggregated / De-identified Data.** We may share aggregated or de-identified data (e.g., "lost pet recovery rates by region") that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.
