## 5. Community Safety & In-Person Meetings

Reuniting with a pet often involves meeting a stranger. **You are solely responsible for your interactions with other users.** We strongly recommend that you:

- Meet in public, well-lit locations (many police stations offer safe-exchange zones);
- Bring another adult with you;
- Verify pet ownership through microchip scans at a veterinarian or shelter before transferring an animal;
- Never send money, gift cards, or "verification codes" to anyone claiming to have found your pet before independently confirming they have the animal (this is a common scam); and
- Report suspicious behavior in-app at [REPORTING PATH — e.g., "the flag icon on any profile or report"] or to [SUPPORT EMAIL].
